Honest questions

Is this a girlfriend / dating sim?

No. Lumi is intentionally a productivity coach — friendly senpai energy, not romantic. She'll redirect if you try to push her that way. We made this call partly for ethics, partly because Stripe/PayPal/Apple kill anything else.

How much does the AI cost me?

You bring your own API key from any of four providers — OpenRouter (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Mistral, DeepSeek and many more), OpenAI direct, Anthropic direct, or Mistral La Plateforme direct. You pay the model provider — usually less than $1/month at normal use. Lumi never sees your key.

What do I actually get for Pro?

Pro ($7/mo) unlocks ElevenLabs anime voices with real amplitude-driven lip-sync (instead of OS voices with simulated mouth animation). You still bring your own ElevenLabs API key — Pro just unlocks the feature in the app. You also get every future Pro feature auto-unlocked, and you're keeping an indie dev fed 🌸

Can I use my own character?

Yes. Lumi reads any standard .vrm file. Drop yours into the app folder, or grab one from VRoid Hub. We're also commissioning premium character packs as DLC.

Does it work offline?

The character + Pomodoro + timer + voice all work offline. Chat needs an internet connection because we route to LLM providers.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything is stored locally on your device. Your API key never leaves your machine. We don't operate any server.

What platforms?

Windows first (live beta), macOS and Linux next. Steam release planned for Q3.
